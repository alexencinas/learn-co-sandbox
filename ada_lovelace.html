Ada Lovelace


The following page consists of information about an important STEM figure: Ada Lovelace.


    Facts:

  • Augusta Ada Byron
  • Lord George Gordon Byron
  • Tutors
  • Mother wanted to keep her from developing her father's personality traits
  • Was forced to lie still for self control
  • Mentor: "father of computer" (Charles Babbage)
  • Descriptions of how codes could be creatd for the device to handle letters and symbols along with numbers
  • Died November 27, 1852


Pictures

Ada is considered to be the First Computer Programmer. Her notes were rediscovered in the 1950's when published in a book by B.V. Bowden.

The U.S. Department of Defense named a computer language Ada after Lovelace.
